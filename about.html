About

Why this exists, and where it's going.

I'm Salah. I want to get into cinematography — not just watch films and admire the shots, but actually learn how to make them. Vision Chronicles is where that starts.

I don't have a crew, a real camera rig, or years of training. What I have is an Osmo Pocket 3, an eye I'm trying to train, and a willingness to post the early, rough stuff instead of waiting until it's "good enough." So this is a journal as much as it's a portfolio — every video here is a step, not a finished product.

The first post is proof of that: a shaky, unplanned clip from the USA-Bosnia game, because the whole point was to just start. From here, the plan is to keep showing up, keep filming, and let the work get better in public instead of in private.

If you're here early, thanks for watching me figure this out.
